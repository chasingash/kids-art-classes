About Artspace

Artspace offers creative and fun-filled solutions to children of all ages ranging from 4-18 years old. Our classes pack a variety of creative goodness to fuel your Childs imagination and improve the mental well-being. Browse through our full range of workshops to find the perfect one that both you and your kids will love. Our aim is to help your children develop their artistic creativity and skills while making new friends who share a similar passionate for Art and Crafts.
